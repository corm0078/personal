DPA Gold

Ecommerce

DPA Gold specializes in seal oil tablets and liquid that provide the best source of Omega-3. This Canadian company came to Pointer looking to improve their online presence with a new Shopify Site.

Along with a small team of designers at Pointer we focused this site on making information accessible and appealing. The biggest challenge was making the site look approachable to its users, we accomplished this by using imagery that reflects the users and soft colours. By using friendly faces and fun texture we were able to avoid making the site look like a pharmaceutical company. The clean text and strong statement keep it looking trustworthy and professional.
