The Pros Closet

Ecommerce

The Pros Closet is a Reseller of gently used high-quality bicycles. They buy, sell and trade used bicycles and parts which mean they have thousands of unique products and Sku's. They previously only had an eBay storefront. The new e-commerce store had to feature multiple different types of products and appeal to their clientele.

Along with the creative director at Pointer we came up with a simplified colour scheme that would appeal to the customers. We updated the brand style to reflect the higher price point, keeping the font clean with a bold style. We made sure the product images were at the forefront of the design because these are used bicycles and clients will mostly be sold based on the condition of the bicycle.
